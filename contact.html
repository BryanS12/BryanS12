My Medical Journey

A wild dream.

Growing up in Canada, things weren't always smooth - I shared one small bedroom with my brother, was outcasted throughout my childhood for my skin, spent hours studying under a single dim reading light, and was bullied for my eczema-a skin condition that left the skin on my neck and joints horribly scarred.

I had one goal in my early life. I wanted to be doctor. I am a current undergraduate student planning on going into the field of medicine. I am attending the University of California, Santa Barbara, majoring in biology. After getting my bachelors, I am going to medical school, to specialize in Dermatology.

The unfortunate reality of the current state of medicine is that becoming a doctor is that going to Medical school is prohibitively expensive. I have calculated that it will cost upwards of 208,940 dollars for my four years in medical school. Any support would be greatly appreciated, and I cannot thank you enough! The funds will go specifically towards the tuition for medical school.
